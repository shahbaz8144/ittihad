What’s New in Memo Details

Document Management System

DMSNewFeatures

Add User

Effortlessly expand your collaborative network. Invite new team members into your workspace, precisely tailor their access permissions, and foster seamless teamwork dynamics.

  1. Click on the "Add User" button a sidebar will open.
  2. Enter the user's email address and select the user.
  3. Optionally, provide a personalized message or instructions.
  4. Click "Submit" to add the user to your workspace.
  5. The added user will get notified.

Edit Reply

Maintain conversational precision with ease. Edit your replies within a 15-minute timeframe, ensuring your messages are finely tuned for clarity and relevance in ongoing discussions

  1. Click on the "Edit" option that appears within 15 minutes of posting it.
  2. Make necessary changes to your reply text.
  3. Review the edited message for accuracy.
  4. Save your changes to update the reply in the conversation thread.

Share

Maximize the impact of your documents with swift dissemination options. Share content instantly across diverse platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and WhatsApp, or generate direct links for widespread accessibility and engagement.

  1. Hover over the "Share" button and select your preferred platform: Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, or Copy Link.
  2. For social media platforms, log in to your account if prompted and customize your post if desired.
  3. Confirm the sharing action to distribute the document or content.
  4. Alternatively, choose "Copy Link" to copy a direct URL to the document.
  5. Paste the link into emails, messages, or other platforms to share it with specific recipients.
